About

The motivation to start chilesoup came from a difficulty of finding fellow musicians who were interested in playing in a casual jam session once a week. People on CraigsList tended to be very serious about music or even professionals looking to make money from playing gigs asap. On the other end of the spectrum, friends and contacts in university who played instruments would initially be excited by the idea, but would drop out once midterms and finals came around. With chilesoup, we were looking to bring together people who want to play music, but who do not necessarily want to commit to playing shows, recording albums or touring.
